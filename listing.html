Don't have a vNIN yet? Here's how to get one

A vNIN is a temporary, shareable version of your NIN that protects your actual NIN number. You can generate one for free in under a minute:

Option A — NIMC Mobile App: Download the NIMC app → tap "Get Virtual NIN" → choose "Input Enterprise short-code" → enter 715461 → tap Submit. Your vNIN will be generated instantly.

Option B — USSD (no app needed): Dial *346*3*Your NIN*715461# and an SMS with your vNIN will be sent to you.
